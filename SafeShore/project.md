Arduino based Shore Safty monitoring and emergency alarm system

# parts
1. arduino * 2
2. rf transmitter
3. rf reciver
4. buzzer *2
5. push to on button * 2
6. 12v led strip / set * 2
7. bc547 transistor * 2
8. 10k resistor * 4
9. 9v battery * 2
10. 9v battery connector * 2
11. some connecting wires
